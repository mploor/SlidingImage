Mayan Adventure Vacation

Southern Mexico

There are many Mayan ruins in southern Mexico. Perhaps the most famous is Chichen Itza. The temple is aligned to create a remarkable snake shadow at the equinox.

Among the other stops in Southern Mexico are:

  • Merida
  • Palenque
  • San Crisobal de las Casas

Guatemala

Guatemala has much natural beauty in addition to many Mayan ruins. The Acatenango volcano is active, with ash explosions occuring every 5 to 10 minutes.

Other stops in Guatemala include:

  • Panajachel
  • Antigua
  • Rio Dulce
  • Flores
  • Tikal

Belize

Belize has more Mayan ruins, impressive caves, and adds beautiful beaches and islands. Caye Caulker is a small and charming island with beautiful snorkeling opportunities and a fun night life.

Some Belize stops:

  • San Ignacio
  • Crystal Caves
  • Caye Caulker
